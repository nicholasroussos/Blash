Gulf Coast Taekwondo Centers

We are a family run business serving the Gulf Coast region for over 20 years. We have two facilities, one is located in Fairhope, AL and the other in Gulf Shores AL. Both adult and children classes are held in either school. Our instructor views Taekwondo as more than just simple self defence. To him it can also be used to develop spiritually. The three points we focus on are: discipline, personal achievement, and confidence. It is our hope that through our schools we can help develop our students.

Mr. Morris is the instructor for both the Fairhope and Gulf Shores school. He has been teaching Taekwondo for 35 years. It has been a labor of love for him. He has trained since 1978. He won a Taekwondo National Championship in 1984. He describes it as the moment that stands out the most during his competing youth. After winning Mr. Morris continued to compete for some years, until he eventually began teaching. Currently, he holds a 7th degree black belt. The three core principles of his practice are: instilling confidence, forming discipline, and achieving the student's best.

Class drills include the following: kicking and punching drills, freestyle and controlled sparring, Taekwondo United form practice, and self defence training. Class is not structured beforehand, instiead it is dynamic.
